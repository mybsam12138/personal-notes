
# Sukiyaki Cooking Guide (Quick Meal Kit)

A simple way to cook a sukiyaki meal kit with beef, vegetables, noodles, and sauce.

Total cooking time: about 5–10 minutes.

---

## Ingredients (Typical Meal Kit)

- Sliced beef
- Vegetable mix (tofu, mushrooms, greens, etc.)
- Udon noodles
- Sukiyaki sauce packet
- Water

---

## Step 1: Prepare the Soup Base

1. Pour the sukiyaki sauce into a pot.
2. Add water in a **1:5 ratio** (1 part sauce, 5 parts water).
3. Heat the pot until the soup **comes to a boil**.

---

## Step 2: Cook the Vegetables

1. Add the vegetable mix to the boiling soup.
2. Cook for **2–3 minutes** until vegetables begin to soften.

---

## Step 3: Add the Noodles

1. Put the udon noodles into the pot.
2. Cook until the soup returns to a boil.
3. Loosen the noodles gently with chopsticks or a spoon.

---

## Step 4: Cook the Beef

1. Add the sliced beef to the boiling soup.
2. Cook for **20–30 seconds** until the beef changes color.

Do not overcook the beef or it may become tough.

---

## Step 5: Serve

1. Turn off the heat.
2. Transfer the cooked ingredients and broth to bowls.
3. Serve immediately while hot.

---

## Tips

- Add vegetables first because they take longer to cook.
- Beef cooks very quickly and should be added last.
- Adjust the amount of water if you prefer a stronger or lighter flavor.
