# Simple Steamed Asparagus with Soy Sauce (Electric Steamer)

## Ingredients

-   200 g fresh asparagus
-   1--2 teaspoons soy sauce
-   1 teaspoon cooking oil (optional)
-   1 clove garlic (optional, minced)
-   Pinch of salt (optional)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Preparation

1.  **Wash the asparagus**
    -   Rinse thoroughly under running water.
2.  **Trim the stems**
    -   Cut off the tough bottom part (about 2--3 cm).
3.  **Optional peeling**
    -   If the asparagus is thick, lightly peel the lower stem with a
        vegetable peeler.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Steaming Method (Electric Steamer)

1.  Fill the electric steamer with water.
2.  Place the asparagus evenly on the steaming tray.
3.  Steam for **5--7 minutes** until the asparagus turns bright green
    and tender.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Seasoning

1.  Transfer the steamed asparagus to a plate.
2.  Drizzle **soy sauce** evenly on top.
3.  Add a small amount of **cooking oil** for aroma.
4.  Optionally sprinkle minced garlic.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Tips

-   Do not overcook asparagus; it should remain **slightly crisp**.
-   Bright green color indicates proper cooking.
-   This dish pairs well with steamed pork ribs and rice.
