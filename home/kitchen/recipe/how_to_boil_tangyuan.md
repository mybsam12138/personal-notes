# How to Boil Tangyuan (Glutinous Rice Balls)

## Ingredients

-   Tangyuan (fresh or frozen)
-   Water

## Equipment

-   Pot
-   Spoon or ladle

## Steps

1.  **Fill the pot with water**
    -   Add enough water so the tangyuan can move freely while cooking.
2.  **Bring the water to a boil**
    -   Heat the water until it reaches a rolling boil.
3.  **Add the tangyuan**
    -   Gently place the tangyuan into the boiling water.
    -   Stir slowly once or twice to prevent them from sticking to the
        bottom of the pot.
4.  **Cook the tangyuan**
    -   Keep the water at a gentle boil.
    -   Cook for about **5 minutes**.
5.  **Stir occasionally**
    -   Lightly stir every minute to prevent sticking.
6.  **Check if they are ready**
    -   When the tangyuan **float to the surface**, they are usually
        cooked.
7.  **Serve**
    -   Remove them with a spoon and serve in hot water or sweet soup.

## Tips to Prevent Sticking

-   Use enough water.
-   Stir gently after adding the tangyuan.
-   Do not overcrowd the pot.
-   Keep the water boiling but not violently.

## Approximate Cooking Time

-   Fresh tangyuan: about **5 minutes**
-   Frozen tangyuan: about **5--7 minutes**
