# Tomato Egg Soup (Quick Home Recipe)

A simple tomato egg soup for **2 people**.\
Total cooking time: **5--8 minutes**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Ingredients

-   2 tomatoes
-   1--2 eggs
-   500--600 ml water
-   1/2 teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)
-   1/4 teaspoon sugar (optional)
-   1 teaspoon cooking oil
-   A few drops of sesame oil (optional)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Preparation

### Wash and Cut Tomatoes

1.  Rinse tomatoes under running water.
2.  Remove the stem area.
3.  Cut each tomato into **6--8 pieces**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Cooking Steps

### 1. Heat the Pot

Add **1 teaspoon cooking oil** to a pot and heat over **medium heat**.

### 2. Cook the Tomatoes

Add the tomato pieces and cook for **1--2 minutes** until: - tomatoes
soften - some juice is released

Optional: add a **small pinch of salt and sugar**.

### 3. Add Water

Pour in **500--600 ml hot water**.

Bring to a boil and cook **1--2 minutes** so the tomato flavor mixes
with the soup.

### 4. Add the Egg

1.  Beat the eggs in a bowl.
2.  When the soup is boiling, slowly pour the egg into the soup.
3.  Gently stir to create **egg ribbons**.

### 5. Season

Add salt to taste and a few drops of sesame oil if desired.

Turn off the heat.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Result

The soup should be: - light red in color - fresh tomato aroma - soft
tomato pieces - delicate egg ribbons
