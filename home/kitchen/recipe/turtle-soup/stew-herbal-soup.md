# Softshell Turtle Herbal Soup (Water Turtle Soup)

This guide describes how to prepare a simple herbal softshell turtle soup using a small **1L double-boiler (water-bath stew pot)**. The portion is suitable for **two people**.

---

# Ingredients

- 1 pack softshell turtle pieces (with pork bones if included)
- 1/2 pack herbal soup mix
    - Ophiopogon (Mai Dong)
    - Dried longan
    - Dried tangerine peel
    - Red dates
- 2 slices fresh ginger
- 2 bowls water (about 600–700 ml)
- Salt (to taste)

---

# Equipment

- Small double-boiler stew pot (1L capacity)
- Inner ceramic pot
- Outer heating pot (water bath)
- Regular pot for blanching

---

# Step 1 – Thaw the Meat

If the turtle pieces are frozen:

1. Keep the meat inside the sealed bag.
2. Submerge the bag in **cold water**.
3. Wait **20–30 minutes** until partially thawed.

The meat does not need to be fully thawed.

---

# Step 2 – Blanch the Turtle Pieces

This step removes blood and reduces fishy smell.

1. Bring a pot of water to boil.
2. Add the turtle pieces.
3. Boil for **2–3 minutes**.
4. Remove and rinse under clean water.

---

# Step 3 – Prepare the Inner Pot

Add the following into the ceramic inner pot:

- Blanched turtle pieces
- 1/2 pack herbal soup mix
- 2 slices ginger
- 2 bowls of water (about 600–700 ml)

The water should **just cover the ingredients**.

Do not fill the pot more than **2/3 full**.

---

# Step 4 – Add Water to the Outer Pot

Pour water into the **outer metal pot** until it reaches about **half the height of the inner pot**.

This water creates the **double-boiling (water bath) heating effect**.

---

# Step 5 – Stew the Soup

Place the inner pot inside the cooker and close the lid.

Set the cooker to:

**Fast Stew / High Stew Mode**

Cooking time:

**1 hour 40 minutes – 2 hours**

---

# Step 6 – Final Seasoning

After cooking:

1. Open the lid.
2. Add a small amount of **salt**.
3. Stir gently.

The soup is now ready to serve.

---

# Expected Result

The soup should be:

- Light brown or amber colored
- Mild herbal aroma
- Clear broth
- Tender turtle meat

This quantity yields **about two bowls of soup**.

---

# Tips

- Do not add too much tangerine peel, as it can make the soup bitter.
- Salt should always be added **after cooking**, not before.
- Double-boiled soup loses very little water, so the initial water amount is close to the final soup volume.

---

# Serving

Serve hot.

Best enjoyed as a **nourishing herbal soup for two people**.
