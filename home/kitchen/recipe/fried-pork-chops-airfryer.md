# Fried Pork Chops (Air Fryer Guide)

A simple way to cook frozen breaded pork chops using an air fryer.

Total cooking time: about **16 minutes**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Ingredients

-   Frozen breaded pork chop
-   Air fryer

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Step 1: Preheat

1.  Preheat the air fryer to **200°C (392°F)**.
2.  Let it heat for **about 1 minute**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Step 2: First Cooking Stage

1.  Place the frozen pork chop directly into the air fryer basket.
2.  Cook at **200°C** for **8 minutes**.

No need to thaw before cooking.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Step 3: Flip the Pork Chop

1.  After 8 minutes, open the air fryer.
2.  Flip the pork chop to cook the other side evenly.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Step 4: Second Cooking Stage

1.  Continue cooking at **200°C** for another **8 minutes**.
2.  The outside should become **golden and crispy**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Step 5: Cut and Serve

1.  Carefully remove the pork chop (it will be hot).
2.  Cut it into strips or bite-sized pieces.
3.  Serve immediately.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Tips

-   Do not thaw the pork chop before cooking.
-   Use tongs or chopsticks when flipping to avoid burns.
-   Cooking time may vary slightly depending on the air fryer model.
-   The pork chop should have a **golden crust and juicy interior** when
    done.
