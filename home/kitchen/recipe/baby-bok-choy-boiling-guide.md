
# How to Boil Baby Bok Choy

A simple method to clean, cut, and boil baby bok choy.

---

## Ingredients

- Baby bok choy (Shanghai bok choy)
- Water
- A pinch of salt (optional)
- A few drops of cooking oil (optional)

---

## Step 1: Clean the Bok Choy

1. Fill a bowl with clean water.
2. Put the bok choy into the water.
3. Gently move them around to remove dirt.
4. Rinse them again under running water.
5. Pay attention to the base where soil may hide.

---

## Step 2: Cut the Bok Choy

1. If they are small, you can cook them whole.
2. If they are larger, cut them **lengthwise in half**.
3. Remove any damaged outer leaves if necessary.

---

## Step 3: Boil Water

1. Fill a pot with enough water to cover the vegetables.
2. Bring the water to a **full boil**.

Optional:
- Add a **small pinch of salt**.
- Add **a few drops of cooking oil** to keep the color bright.

---

## Step 4: Boil the Bok Choy

1. Put the bok choy into the boiling water.
2. Cook for **1–2 minutes**.

The stems should become tender but still slightly crisp.

---

## Step 5: Remove and Serve

1. Remove the bok choy from the pot.
2. Drain the water.
3. Serve immediately.

Optional seasoning:
- Soy sauce
- Oyster sauce
- Sesame oil
