# Steamed Pork Ribs with Soybean Paste (Using Electric Steamer)

## Ingredients

-   400 g pork ribs (cut into small pieces)
-   1 tablespoon soybean paste
-   1 teaspoon soy sauce
-   2 cloves garlic (minced)
-   1/2 teaspoon sugar
-   1 teaspoon cornstarch
-   1 teaspoon cooking oil
-   Optional: chopped green onion

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Preparation

1.  **Clean the ribs**
    -   Rinse the pork ribs under cold water.
    -   Drain well.
2.  **Marinate the ribs**
    -   Add the following into a bowl:
        -   soybean paste
        -   soy sauce
        -   minced garlic
        -   sugar
        -   cornstarch
        -   cooking oil
    -   Mix thoroughly so every piece is coated.
    -   Marinate for **20 minutes**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Steaming Method (Electric Steamer)

1.  Add water to the base of the electric steamer.
2.  Place the marinated ribs on a plate.
3.  Put the plate into the steamer tray.
4.  Steam for **15--18 minutes** after the water starts boiling.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Finishing

1.  Carefully remove the plate from the steamer.
2.  Sprinkle chopped green onion if available.
3.  Serve hot with steamed rice.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Tips

-   Cutting ribs into **small pieces (3--4 cm)** improves tenderness.
-   Cornstarch helps keep the meat **soft and juicy**.
-   Do not over-steam; the ribs may become dry.
