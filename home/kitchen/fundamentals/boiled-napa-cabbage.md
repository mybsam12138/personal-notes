# How to Wash and Boil Baby Napa Cabbage (400g)



## Portion



For **2 people**, 400 g of baby napa cabbage is a suitable amount.



Typical vegetable portion:



| People | Amount |

|------|------|

| 1 person | ~200 g |

| 2 people | ~400 g |



You can cook **all of it** for two people.



---



# Step 1 — Wash the Cabbage



1. Remove the cabbage from the plastic package.

2. Cut off the bottom root (about **0.5–1 cm**) if needed.

3. Slightly separate the leaves with your hands.

4. Place the cabbage in a bowl of clean water.

5. Soak for **30–60 seconds** and gently move the leaves to remove dirt.

6. Rinse once more under running water.



Tip: Baby napa cabbage is usually quite clean, so **1–2 rinses are enough**.



---



# Step 2 — Cut the Cabbage



You can cook it **whole or cut**.



Recommended method:



- Cut each cabbage **lengthwise into halves**

- Or cut into **3–4 sections**



This helps it cook evenly and makes it easier to eat.



---



# Step 3 — Boil the Cabbage



1. Add water to a pot.

2. Bring the water to a **full boil**.

3. Add:

    - a **small pinch of salt**

    - **a few drops of oil** (optional, keeps color bright)

4. Put the cabbage into the boiling water.

5. Boil for **2–3 minutes**.



Remove when the leaves become **soft and slightly translucent**.



Do not overcook, or it may become too soft.



---



# Simple Seasoning Ideas



## Option 1 — Very Simple

- Soy sauce

- A few drops of sesame oil



## Option 2 — Garlic Flavor

1. Heat a small amount of oil.

2. Add chopped garlic.

3. Pour the hot garlic oil over the cabbage.

4. Add soy sauce if desired.
