# Kitchen Devices Summary

## 1. Air Fryer

### Overview

The air fryer is used for quick, oil-free cooking with hot air
circulation. It is suitable for small household use (1--2 people).

### Capacity (Volume)

- **Total Capacity: 7.0L**
- Dual-layer internal rack (upper and lower levels)
- Suitable for medium household cooking (2–4 people)
- 23*22cm

**Practical cooking examples:**

- 10–14 chicken wings (single layer)
- 3–4 medium sweet potatoes
- One small whole chicken (depending on size)
- 8-inch pizza
- Multiple servings of frozen snacks
- Rice patties or reheated fried food (can use dual layers)

> Note: Although the total volume is 7L, effective cooking capacity depends on airflow clearance.
>  Overloading may reduce crispness and cooking efficiency.

### Cooking Methods

-   Air frying
-   Reheating crispy food
-   Light roasting
-   Baking small items

### Advantages

-   Low oil usage
-   Crispy exterior texture
-   Convenient for frozen or semi-prepared food
-   Simple operation

### Limitations

-   Limited batch size
-   Not ideal for soups or liquid-based cooking
-   Requires basket cleaning after oily food
