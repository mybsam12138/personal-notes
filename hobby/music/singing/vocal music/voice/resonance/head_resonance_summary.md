# Head Resonance — Summary Guide

## 1. What Is Head Resonance?

Head resonance is the **sensation of vibration in the face and head area** during singing, especially in higher pitches.

👉 It is NOT actual airflow going into the head.

---

## 2. What Actually Happens Physically

### Airflow (real path)
- Lungs → vocal cords → vocal tract → mouth
- Soft palate is usually **lifted**
- Airflow goes mainly through the **oral cavity**

❌ Air does NOT go into:
- the skull
- the brain
- the head cavities

---

### Vibration (what you feel)

- Vocal cords create sound vibration
- Vibration travels through:
  - air column
  - bones
  - tissues

👉 You may feel vibration in:
- nose bridge
- cheekbones
- forehead

---

## 3. Key Distinction

| Concept | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Airflow | Physical movement of air |
| Resonance | Amplification and shaping of sound |
| Head resonance | Sensation of vibration in head area |

---

## 4. Why High Notes Feel Like “Head”

- Vocal cords become thinner and more stretched
- Sound becomes lighter and more efficient
- Resonance shifts forward (perceptually)

👉 Result:
- Less “chest” feeling
- More “head” sensation

---

## 5. Role of the Soft Palate

- **Raised soft palate** → airflow stays in mouth
- **Lowered soft palate** → airflow can enter nasal cavity

👉 In good singing (vowels):
- Soft palate is **raised**
- Nasal airflow is minimal

---

## 6. Common Misunderstandings

❌ “Send the sound into the head”
❌ “Use the nose for high notes”
❌ “More nasal = better resonance”

---

## 7. Correct Understanding

> Head resonance is a RESULT of efficient coordination, not something you directly control.

It comes from:
- Efficient vocal cord vibration
- Proper vocal tract shape
- Stable airflow

---

## 8. How to Develop Head Resonance

### Step 1: Humming
- Use “mmm” or “ng”
- Feel vibration in face

### Step 2: Open to vowel
- “mmm → ah”
- Keep the forward vibration

### Step 3: Light coordination
- Think “lighter”, not “stronger”

### Step 4: Maintain airflow
- Steady, not pushed

---

## 9. Final Summary

Head resonance is not about directing airflow into the head. It is the natural sensation of vibration in the facial and head areas caused by efficient vocal production and resonance shaping.

---

## 10. One Sentence

> You don’t send air to your head—you allow vibration to be felt there.
