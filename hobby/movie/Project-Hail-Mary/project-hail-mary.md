# Project Hail Mary — Main Storyline Summary

## Basic Setup

Ryland Grace, a former science teacher, wakes up alone on a spaceship with no memory of who he is or why he is there.

As his memory gradually returns, he realizes:
- He is the sole survivor of a space mission
- His mission is to save Earth from an extinction-level threat

---

## The Crisis

A mysterious microorganism is infecting stars, including the Sun, causing them to lose energy.

This threatens all life on Earth.

Humanity launches a desperate mission:
- Send a spacecraft to another star system
- Find a solution before Earth freezes

---

## The Mission

Grace is sent into deep space as part of this last attempt.

However:
- His crewmates die during the journey
- He must complete the mission alone

As he regains his memory, he understands:
- He was not originally a willing hero
- He was forced into the mission due to his expertise

---

## First Contact

While investigating the problem, Grace encounters:
- An alien life form from another civilization

This alien:
- Is also trying to save its own species
- Becomes an unexpected ally

They develop:
- A unique communication system
- A cooperative scientific partnership

---

## Rising Action

Together, they:
- Study the star-killing organism
- Develop a way to stop it
- Share knowledge across species

Their friendship becomes a central emotional element.

---

## Climax

Grace must make a critical choice:
- Return to Earth and complete his mission
- Or stay behind to help his alien friend survive

He ultimately chooses:
- To sacrifice his return to Earth

---

## Ending

Grace helps save both civilizations:
- Earth is given the solution to survive
- The alien species is also saved

Grace remains in space, living among the alien civilization.

---

## Themes

- Sacrifice and responsibility
- Survival of humanity
- Cooperation across species
- Science and problem-solving
- Personal growth and redemption

---

## One-Line Summary

A reluctant astronaut becomes humanity’s last hope and, through an unexpected friendship, helps save two worlds.
