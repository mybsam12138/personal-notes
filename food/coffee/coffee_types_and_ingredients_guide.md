
# Coffee Shop Coffee Types and Ingredients Guide

## 1. Basic Components of Coffee Drinks

Most coffee shop drinks are made from a combination of these ingredients:

### Espresso
- A concentrated coffee extracted with high pressure.
- Strong flavor and thick body.
- Most coffee drinks are based on espresso.

### Milk
- Adds smoothness and reduces bitterness.
- Can be steamed, foamed, or cold.

### Milk Foam
- Light foam created by steaming milk.
- Changes the texture of the drink.

### Water
- Used to dilute espresso (for example, Americano).

### Syrups / Flavorings
- Examples: vanilla, caramel, hazelnut, chocolate.

---

# 2. Common Coffee Types

## Espresso
**Ingredients**
- 1 shot espresso

**Characteristics**
- Very strong
- Small volume
- Intense flavor

**Best for**
People who enjoy strong coffee taste.

---

## Americano
**Ingredients**
- 1–2 shots espresso
- Hot water

**Characteristics**
- Similar strength to regular brewed coffee
- Smooth and less intense than espresso

**Best for**
People who want coffee flavor without too much milk.

---

## Latte
**Ingredients**
- 1 shot espresso
- Large amount of steamed milk
- Thin milk foam

**Characteristics**
- Mild coffee taste
- Creamy texture

**Best for**
Beginners or people who prefer smooth coffee.

---

## Cappuccino
**Ingredients**
- 1 shot espresso
- Steamed milk
- Thick milk foam

**Characteristics**
- Balanced coffee and milk flavor
- More foam than latte

**Best for**
People who want both milk texture and coffee flavor.

---

## Flat White
**Ingredients**
- 1–2 shots espresso
- Steamed milk (very thin foam)

**Characteristics**
- Stronger coffee flavor than latte
- Smooth texture

**Best for**
People who like milk coffee but stronger taste.

---

## Mocha
**Ingredients**
- Espresso
- Chocolate syrup
- Steamed milk
- Sometimes whipped cream

**Characteristics**
- Sweet chocolate flavor
- Dessert-like drink

**Best for**
People who like sweet coffee.

---

## Macchiato
**Ingredients**
- Espresso
- Small amount of milk foam

**Characteristics**
- Mostly espresso
- Slight milk texture

**Best for**
People who want strong coffee but a little smoother.

---

# 3. How to Choose Coffee You Like

## Step 1: Decide Coffee Strength

Strong coffee flavor:
- Espresso
- Macchiato
- Flat White

Medium strength:
- Americano
- Cappuccino

Mild coffee flavor:
- Latte
- Mocha

---

## Step 2: Decide Milk Preference

No milk:
- Espresso
- Americano

Some milk:
- Flat White
- Cappuccino

Lots of milk:
- Latte
- Mocha

---

## Step 3: Decide Sweetness

Not sweet:
- Espresso
- Americano
- Flat White

Slightly sweet:
- Latte

Sweet:
- Mocha
- Flavored latte

---

# 4. Simple Coffee Selection Guide

If you like strong coffee → Espresso or Americano

If you like smooth coffee → Latte

If you like balanced coffee and milk → Cappuccino

If you like strong coffee with milk → Flat White

If you like sweet coffee → Mocha

---

# 5. Tips for Beginners

Start with:
- Latte
- Cappuccino
- Mocha

Then try stronger drinks:
- Americano
- Flat White

Finally try pure coffee:
- Espresso

This progression helps your taste adapt to stronger coffee flavors.
