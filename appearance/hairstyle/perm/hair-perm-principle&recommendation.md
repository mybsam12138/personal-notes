# Hair Perm Principles & Recommendation

## 1. Principle of Perm (How Perm Works)

Hair is mainly composed of **keratin protein**, which contains different types of chemical bonds:

- **Disulfide bonds (strong)** → determine permanent hair shape
- **Hydrogen bonds (weak)** → determine temporary shape

### Perm Process

1. Hair is wrapped on rods (defines target shape)
2. Chemical solution breaks **disulfide bonds**
3. Hair becomes flexible and reshaped
4. Neutralizer reforms disulfide bonds in new shape

### Key Idea

- Perm = **rearranging disulfide bonds**
- Not destroying hair, but **changing internal structure**

---

## 2. Cold Perm vs Heat Perm

### Cold Perm

- Uses: chemical solution only
- Condition: wet hair
- Result:
    - tighter curls
    - more volume / fluffiness
    - less control

**Characteristics:**
- More random texture
- Easier to become frizzy
- Lower stability

---

### Heat Perm 

- Uses: chemical + heat
- Condition: semi-dry hair
- Result:
    - soft, natural waves
    - smoother finish
    - better control

**Characteristics:**
- More precise shaping
- More stable structure
- Less frizz

---

### Key Differences

| Feature | Cold Perm | Heat Perm |
|--------|----------|----------|
| Bond breaking | Chemical | Chemical |
| Heat used | ❌ | ✅ |
| Control | Low | High |
| Texture | Fluffy / random | Smooth / natural |
| Stability | Medium | High |

---

## 3. Types of Perm

### 1. Full Texture Perm 

- Adds:
    - curls
    - volume
    - texture
- Risk:
    - too fluffy
    - messy look

---

### 2. Heat / Digital Perm 

- Adds:
    - controlled curls
    - smooth waves
- Suitable for:
    - longer hair
    - styled looks

---

### 3. Root Perm 

- Focus:
    - hair roots only
- Adds:
    - root lift
    - volume support
- No visible curls

---

## 4. Why Hair Becomes Flat Over Time

Main reasons:

1. Hair grows longer → heavier → pulled down
2. Straight hair → less natural volume
3. Scalp oil (sebum) → makes hair stick to scalp
4. Haircut structure weakens over time

**Conclusion:**
Flat hair = loss of **root support**

---

## 5. Why Root Cold Perm Suits You

### Your Situation

- Hair is:

    - natural appearance
- Prevents:
    - hair sticking to scalp

---

- No obvious curls
- Easy daily maintenance
- Solves flattening problem directly
