# Foil vs Rotary Electric Shavers

## Overview

Electric shavers generally fall into two main categories:

1.  **Foil Shavers**
2.  **Rotary Shavers**

Both use an outer guard (foil or head) and an inner blade, but the
**cutting mechanism and shaving motion are different**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Foil Shaver

## Structure

-   Thin **metal foil layer**
-   **Oscillating blades** underneath
-   Usually 1--3 foil heads aligned in a straight line

```{=html}
<!-- -->
```
    Foil (outer guard)
           ↓
    Oscillating blade (left ↔ right)

## How It Works

1.  Beard enters holes in the foil.
2.  Inner blades move rapidly side‑to‑side.
3.  Hair is cut at skin level.

Typical blade speed can exceed **10,000 oscillations per minute**.

## Shaving Motion

Foil shavers work best with **straight movements**.

Example:

    Up / Down motion
    ↑ ↓ ↑ ↓

## Advantages

-   Very **close shave**
-   **Fast cutting speed**
-   Good for **short or straight beard growth**
-   Precise edges (sideburns, mustache)

## Disadvantages

-   Can cause **more skin irritation**
-   Less flexible on uneven facial contours
-   Works best when beard is **short**

## Typical Brands

-   Braun
-   Panasonic

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Rotary Shaver

## Structure

-   Usually **three circular shaving heads**
-   Each head contains **rotating blades**
-   Heads move independently to follow face shape

```{=html}
<!-- -->
```
    Circular guard
         ↓
    Rotating blade

## How It Works

1.  Hair enters small holes in the circular guard.
2.  Inner blades rotate continuously.
3.  Hair is cut as blades spin under the guard.

## Shaving Motion

Rotary shavers work best with **circular motion**.

Example:

    Circular motion
    ↺ ↺ ↺

## Advantages

-   **Comfortable shaving**
-   Better for **curved facial areas**
-   Works well on **longer or irregular beard growth**
-   Usually **less skin irritation**

## Disadvantages

-   Not as close as foil shaving
-   Slightly slower shaving speed

## Typical Brand

-   Philips

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Comparison

  Feature           Foil Shaver                Rotary Shaver
  ----------------- -------------------------- --------------------
  Blade Motion      Side-to-side oscillation   Circular rotation
  Shaving Motion    Straight lines             Circular motion
  Shave Closeness   Very close                 Moderate
  Comfort           Medium                     High
  Best Beard Type   Short / straight           Longer / irregular
  Face Contours     Moderate                   Excellent
  Typical Brands    Braun, Panasonic           Philips

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Simple Selection Guide

Choose **Foil Shaver** if:

-   You want the **closest shave**
-   Your beard grows **mostly straight**
-   You shave **daily**

Choose **Rotary Shaver** if:

-   Your beard grows in **different directions**
-   Your skin is **sensitive**
-   You prefer a **more comfortable shave**
